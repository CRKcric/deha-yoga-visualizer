About Deha

Built for the way you move

Most people who practise yoga or exercise at home have no way of knowing whether their posture is actually correct. A slight forward lean, a collapsed knee, an uneven hip — these small misalignments are nearly invisible to the person making them, yet they build up over weeks into strain, discomfort, or injury.

Deha was built to close that gap. Using your device's camera, it maps your body position against reference pose data and delivers precise, live corrections — the kind of feedback you'd normally only get from a watchful instructor. No subscription, no account, no wearable device needed.

This is an early version. What's here today is the core correction engine. We're working toward session history, progress tracking across poses, and personalised difficulty curves — a full companion for anyone who wants to move better, on their own terms.
